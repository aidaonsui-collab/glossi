Messaging Policy

SMS Terms.

How Glossi's text-message notifications work — what we send, how often, and how to stop.

Last updated June 8, 2026.

Program name

Glossi SMS Notifications — operated by Glossi, a beauty-services marketplace serving the Rio Grande Valley, Texas.

What messages you'll receive

If you opt in, Glossi sends transactional text messages tied to your account activity:

  • Booking confirmations — when an appointment is confirmed.
  • Bid alerts — when a salon responds to a service request you posted.
  • Appointment reminders — ahead of a scheduled visit.
  • Booking updates — reschedules, cancellations, and related changes.

These are notifications about your own bookings and bids. Glossi does not sell or share your phone number, and does not send third-party marketing.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies by your account activity — typically 1–5 messages per booking.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply. Glossi does not charge for these messages; standard rates from your mobile carrier apply.

How to opt in

You opt in by checking a dedicated SMS-consent checkbox when you create an account at www.glossi.cc/signup. The checkbox is separate from the Terms of Service agreement and is not pre-checked. It reads:

"I agree to receive text messages from Glossi about my bookings, bids, and appointments. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for help."

Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of creating an account or of any purchase.

How to opt out and get help

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe at any time. You will receive one confirmation message and then no further texts.

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly using the details below.

Support contact

Email support@glossi.cc or visit www.glossi.cc/help. We're a small team based in McAllen, Texas, and reply within a few hours on weekdays.

Privacy

Your information is handled per our Privacy Policy. We do not sell your data or share your phone number with salons until you book. No mobile opt-in information is shared with third parties for their own marketing.
